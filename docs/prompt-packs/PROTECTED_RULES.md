# Protected Rules

These are permanent business and architecture rules for the Shipwrecked Pools app.

1. The pool outline is not decorative. It must stay connected to operational data: service markers, reports, photos, service visits, chemistry where relevant, quotes, repairs, comments, recommendations, and status history.
2. Customers never see admin-only internal notes.
3. Technicians never see billing, payment details, customer profitability, route profitability, quote margins, or sensitive business data.
4. Route progress must protect privacy. Customers see progress, ETA, and stops-before-you, not other customer addresses or unnecessary off-route technician movement.
5. Weekly/biweekly recurring services stay on the same weekday unless admin intentionally overrides.
6. Commercial property-management users may log permitted daily chemical readings and chemicals added, but do not gain broader admin access.
7. High-quality media is retained for up to two years, then compressed/archive-optimized after two years.
8. Account deletion removes pool-owner-specific account info except historical photos and report logs retained for liability.
9. V1 customer questions are answered by humans, not AI.
10. Quote approvals require approval action, checkbox confirmation, typed signature, quote version, payment event, and audit log.
11. Failed-payment workflows must be operationally enforceable, including customer suspension and technician skip-service notification after configured threshold.
12. Repair/maintenance workflows remain single-technician for now.
13. Technician accountability metrics are admin-only and must not become customer-visible quality scoring.
14. Weather intelligence may affect notifications, route estimates, and service decisions, but must not expose unsafe or confusing information to customers.
